Ayokong isipin mo na nagbibilang ako ng pagkukulang mo o sinisisi kita, gusto ko lang talaga maging tapat kasi magka-partner tayo sa lahat.

You mean the absolute world to me, my comfort person, and seeing you upset is the last thing I ever want. I value everything about us so much.
